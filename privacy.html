Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026 · Last updated: July 6, 2026
The short version: we collect what we need to run the app, we use it to make your experience better, and we never sell your data. Full details below.

1. Who We Are

Studlin Labs, Inc. ("Studlin," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Studlin website and application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and who we share it with.

Questions? Email us at studing2026@gmail.com.

2. What We Collect

Account data

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials. Passwords are never stored by Studlin directly. Authentication is handled securely through our authentication provider. If you sign in with a third-party account, we receive only the information you permit, such as your name, email, and profile photo.

Usage data

We collect information about how you use the Service: which features you access, how long you study, your streak history, tasks you create, flashcards you generate, calendar events, and similar activity. This powers your study stats, weekly summary, streak tracking, and AI scheduling features.

AI content

When you use the AI tutor, upload notes or PDFs, or interact with any AI powered feature, the content you submit is sent to our AI provider to generate a response. We do not use your submitted content to train AI models beyond what is necessary to produce your response in that session.

Payment data

If you subscribe to a paid plan, payment is processed by our third-party payment processor. Studlin never receives or stores your full card number. We receive only limited billing metadata (last four digits, card type, and subscription status) necessary to manage your account.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • create and maintain your account
  • power AI features including the tutor, scheduler, and flashcard generator
  • calculate streaks, leaderboard rankings, and study metrics
  • process payments and manage your subscription
  • send service communications and reminders you request
  • keep the Service secure and diagnose technical problems

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with advertisers. We do not use your data for purposes unrelated to operating Studlin.

4. Who We Share Your Data With

We share data only with trusted infrastructure providers that are necessary to run the Service. This includes services for authentication, database storage, payment processing, transactional email, and anonymized product analytics. Each provider processes your data strictly to perform its function and is not permitted to use it for their own advertising or to sell it to third parties.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with advertisers or any party for marketing purposes.

5. Education Records and FERPA

Studlin is a voluntary productivity tool. Students enter their own study goals, tasks, and notes. We do not access, request, or store official education records such as transcripts, grades issued by a school, or any records covered by the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA).

If we ever pursue integrations with school systems or learning management platforms, we will update this policy and obtain appropriate legal guidance before doing so.

6. Age Requirements

Studlin is open to all students. There is no minimum age to use the Service. If you are under 18 and wish to subscribe to a paid plan, parental or guardian consent is required before completing a purchase.

If you believe your child has created an account or made a purchase without your consent, contact us at studing2026@gmail.com and we will resolve it promptly.

7. Data Retention and Security

Your data is stored securely with TLS encryption in transit and encryption at rest. We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service.

If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within a commercially reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. To make a request, email us at studing2026@gmail.com.

California residents: we do not sell personal information, so most CCPA opt out rights do not apply to Studlin. We will comply with all applicable requests under California and other state privacy laws.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email or through the Service. Your continued use after the effective date means you accept the updated policy.
